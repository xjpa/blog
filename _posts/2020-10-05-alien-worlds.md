What if Planets Are Alive?

Man, I had this 420 moment earlier while I was walking my dog.

Where are the aliens in the universe?

There's the [Fermi Paradox](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fermi_paradox), there's the [Dark Forest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dark_forest_hypothesis), there's the [Zookeeper Hypothesis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zoo_hypothesis).

<!-- more -->

And many more.

But what if we're looking in the wrong place? We have anthropomorphized them. Aliens could very well be something we just don't know.

[They could be PLANETS!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wHDefhJ6ROI&t=134s)

Think about it. The same way we don't perceive the bacteria on our skin, what if we're all just the bacteria of this giant body of intelligence. Like we're just some celestial fungus stuck onto this giant sphere dancing across space.

I forgot what sci-fi book I read it was, but it was interesting in how it portrayed planets as just a giant CPU. Thinking about it, that could very well be the home or model for higher intelligence.

Like imagine billions of years into the future, you've solved everything that physics and biology allow, extreme longevity spanning eons. But now you're bored. You just want to play until entropy takes over. So what do you do? You [wirehead](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wirehead_(science_fiction)>) yourself to some powerful quantum supercomputer, interfaced with a [Matrioshka brain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matrioshka_brain) feeding off the energy from the core, utilizing quantum entanglement and superdense coding to simulate everything and play ANY GAME or reality you want. You can have fun simulating anything, you can conduct experiments in theoretical physics, testing the limits of string theory, exploring the nuances of dark matter and dark energy, and even simulating alternate dimensions as proposed in [M-theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/M-theory). You hunker down beneath some diamonds and miles of earth and rock, and lava at extreme pressure surrounding your core like a sphere to protect yourself.

This shell of protection, or what we know of as 'Earth', is not just a physical barrier with shields against cosmic radiation, but also a masterpiece of nanotechnology. Laced with self-repairing automatons (humans or whatever civilization that came before or after us). And despite the wars that ravage the planet, they are all internally programmed to unite when events come to save their home for survival. Then you, the being thousands of subterranean miles inside the shell would just play on that virtual landscape of dreams with no interruption until the computer alerts you to pack your bags before the nearby sun goes supernova or some cataclysmic event goes off.

Anyway, thanks for reading my shitty blog post.

I'm just really bored with these covid lockdowns going on

But these days it does feel like an alien world. There's barely anyone in the streets and malls.

I wonder how the future will be
