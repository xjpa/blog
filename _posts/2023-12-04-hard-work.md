There's this saying:

> No one on his deathbed ever said, I wish I had spent more time grinding

The end mostly varies, it's either "more time working" or "more time on my business" or "more time at the office." But you get the picture... It's about spending more time at work.

<!-- more -->

Charlie Munger, a titan of business and investing, has passed away recently. [News of his death even reached the front page of Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=38451278). He made impact everywhere and worked like a bull.

Did he ever regret spending so much time grinding? Go ahead and google [his last interview](https://www.cnbc.com/2023/11/30/full-transcript-from-cnbcs-charlie-munger-a-life-of-wit-and-wisdom-.html) and he'll say that his biggest regret was not starting the grind earlier, not putting in enough time.

<img src="https://xjpa-assets-2023.netlify.app/bearblog/hard-work-munger.png">

People at the top just have a diff perspective on hard work. I could give like a zillion other examples of people at the top who were still grinding when they knew they were dying: Einstein, Churchill, Edison, Picasso, Steve Jobs.

Mostly, the only people saying "nobody ever said they wish they worked more on their deathbed" are those who have yet to stare death at the face

The recent [Napoleon Bonaparte movie](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LIsfMO5Jd_w) reminded me of his last words:

> "La France, l'armee, Josephine" (France, The Army, Josephine)

source: [The Lost Memoirs of Louis-Joseph Marchand](https://www.amazon.com/Napoleons-Shadow-Louis-Joseph-Marchand-1811-1821/dp/1784382892)

When you're dying, you think about your legacy.

You'll be wishing that you worked harder, to leave behind something so much better

Just like the eternal accelerating expansion of the universe and dark energy, embrace that life is about constant movement.

Accept the constant dance and the demands of hard work

We dont worry about the [Hedonic treadmill](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hedonic_treadmill)

For we're in this ride til the end
