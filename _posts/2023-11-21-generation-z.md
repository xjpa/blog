To my brothers and sisters in the trenches:

The feast is over; the easy cash, the free lunch, gone.

Our playground is a battleground now, and it's everyone for themselves.

<!-- more -->

The jobs your parents knew? Kiss it goodbye.

Startups and VCs, the once promised land, now just smoke and embers.

Banks, too big to fail—until they're not.

The future? A big fat question mark.

So, what's it gonna be?

Lie down, roll over? Or stand up, fight back?

Resumes stacked high, stock options ablaze, big tech's promise—a mirage.

People padded resumes with the fluff of times, chased the dangling carrot

Is that your endgame?

Hell no. We're better than this.

Our talent? Unmatched. Our spirit? Unbroken.

Comfort? Forget it. It's time for guts, for glory.

It's time to be more.

Hear this, I'm calling you out:

Rally the brave. Ignite that genius.

Forge in the fire of this chaos.

It's time to build, friends.

Not tomorrow. Today.

We are the final echo of a bygone era, the ones to usher in the dawn.

The last of the 90s, first of the 2000s, the bridge between centuries.

Embrace the fire.

We have a latent beast caged by convention, but ready. A hunger in our bellies that comfort never filled—a craving for the taste of something real, something wrought by our own hands.

It is time to build.

> **It's the dawn of the twilight age Bjorn. If we're going out, we might as well go out fighting**
